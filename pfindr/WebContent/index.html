PhenoExplorer

A Web Tool for Cross-study Phenotype Identification in dbGaP and Elsewhere

Lack of standardization makes locating and categorizing previously measured variables difficult. We are creating a query tool for biomedical researchers to identify studies and phenotype variables of interest.

The PhenoExplorer Project is funded by NHLBI's pFINDR  program: Phenotype Finder IN Data Resources under grant number 1UH2HL108780.

Explore the site for further information, and please contact us at info@phenoexplorer.org  if you have questions or need help!
